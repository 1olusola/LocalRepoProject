3-Ingredient Pasta

Instructions

  1. 1

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.

  2. 2

    While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant but not browned.

  3. 3

    Drain pasta, reserving ½ cup pasta water. Toss pasta into the skillet with garlic oil. Add a splash of reserved water and mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Chef Tips: Use good-quality olive oil for the best flavor. Add chili flakes for a spicy kick.
